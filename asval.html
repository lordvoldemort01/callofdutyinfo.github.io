Description :


The AS VAL is an integrally suppressed assault rifle introduced in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare and later added to Call of Duty: Mobile.

In multiplayer, the AS VAL is somewhat weak compared to its peers, and also features projectiles, which affect when the bullets actually come into contact with the target. However, the rapid fire rate is exceptional, allowing it to perform well at close and medium range. However, at longer ranges, the uncontrollable recoil may get the better of the player, and send the gun flying upwards.

The 10-Round FMJ attachment changes the fire-mode to semi-automatic, like the SKS, also increasing bullet damage, bullet penetration, and control. However, it will take, at the very least, 3 shots to kill, which is not only weak, but is detrimental, considering the 10-rounds in the magazine with this attachment, though it can kill instantly to the head.
