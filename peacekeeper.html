Description :


The Peacekeeper MK2 is an assault rifle featured in Call of Duty: Black Ops III and Call of Duty: Mobile.

The Peacekeeper MK2 is the successor to the Peacekeeper from Call of Duty: Black Ops II. The most notable change being that the Peacekeeper MK2 is an Assault Rifle, while the original Peacekeeper is a Submachine Gun. Other changes include a slightly higher magazine size, slower rate of fire which it makes up for with a slightly higher amount of damage, faster reload time, and longer range.
